About

Helen

A nature and portrait photographer based in Florida, drawn to the raw, unscripted moments where the natural world reveals itself.

Her work spans landscapes, wildlife, and intimate human portraits — always searching for the stillness between motion, the light that lingers just before it fades.

With a background in environmental studies and a lifelong love of travel, she brings a quiet intentionality to every frame. Her images have taken her from the volcanic shores of Hawai'i to the sequoia groves of California, from the jungles of Southeast Asia to the rose gardens of the Pacific Northwest.
